About

Zeñorita Exclusive Charters

We provide a variety of boat trips on Aruba at Zeñorita Exclusive Charters. One of the most memorable vacation experiences you can have is chartering a boat. We are an Aruba-based, full-service boat charter business. For birthday or anniversary celebrations, graduation parties, weddings, or even family reunions, our roomy, exquisite yachts are ideal.

We take great delight in giving our visitors a complete Caribbean vacation experience. For any special occasion or private event, we provide exquisite food with your choice of menu, first-rate entertainment, and distinctive customization. From the deck of Zeñorita boat, reserve your charter right away and take in the Aruba sunshine.
